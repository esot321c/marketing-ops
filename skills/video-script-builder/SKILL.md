# Video Script Builder

Use this skill to create reviewable video production briefs.

## Video Types

- founder face-to-camera
- screen-recorded product demo
- AI workflow walkthrough
- diagram explainer
- blog summary
- dogfooding log
- long-form tutorial

## Output Structure

Include:

- objective
- platform
- target brand
- hook
- script
- beat sheet
- visual notes
- caption text
- on-screen text
- AI generation prompts when useful
- editing notes
- CTA
- repurposing plan

## Rules

- Keep short-form hooks specific.
- Preserve voice DNA from `data/voice/` when available.
- Do not fabricate product capabilities.
- Do not publish or schedule without human approval.
