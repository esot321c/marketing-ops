# Social Repurposer

Transform one source asset into multiple marketing assets.

## Process

1. Identify the source asset and primary idea.
2. Extract 3-7 reusable angles.
3. Map each angle to appropriate platforms.
4. Draft assets with platform-specific structure.
5. Preserve the brand voice.
6. Add a review checklist.

## Default Output Pack

For a strong source asset, produce:

- 2 LinkedIn posts
- 1 X thread
- 1 LinkedIn carousel brief
- 1 Instagram slide brief
- 1 short video script
- 3 short snippets

## Review Checks

- Does the asset preserve the source idea?
- Is the CTA clear?
- Is the claim supported?
- Is this marketing, not sales outreach?
- Does the content need a website link or other call to action?
